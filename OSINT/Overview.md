# Overview
The purpose of this project is to encourage you to experiment with OSINT in the real world. Screenshots-Graphical recreations, etc., are encouraged with the goal of creating a portfolio piece that showcases your OSINT skills to future employers.
#### Deliverables ####
The E-Portfolio should include at least five pages:
  * OSINT Preparation
  * OSINT Resources & Techniques
  *  Leaks, Breaches, Logs, & Ransomware
  *  OSINT Methodology
  *  Final Reflections
