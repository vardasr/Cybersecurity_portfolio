# Overview

Cybersecurity Awareness

**Objective:** Assess your personal data exposure and its implications for OSINT.

**Task:**
  * Create a section titled "Have I Been Hacked?" for your portfolio
  * Visit Have I Been Pwned? and check if your email address(es) or phone number(s) have been part of a data breach.
  * Document the results, including the number and types of breaches (if any). Use screenshots if they are appropriate
  * Reflect on the potential impacts of these breaches on personal and professional security.
  * Develop actionable recommendations for improving cybersecurity practices, such as using password managers, enabling multi-factor authentication, or monitoring accounts for suspicious activity.
